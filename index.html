The Studio

For over a decade I've filmed weddings and edited hundreds of hours of vows, speeches and toasts — learning exactly where a story lives inside a long recording.

That experience became Corona Apps: a small, independent studio building focused macOS tools for people who work with real images and real voices — photographers, filmmakers and anyone who needs to find the moment that matters.

The story is already in the recording — you just need a faster way to cut it out.
